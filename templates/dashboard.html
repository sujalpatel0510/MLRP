Students Leave Policy

1. The 75% Attendance Rule

  • Minimum Requirement: Students are typically required to maintain at least 75% attendance in each subject to be eligible for semester/final examinations.
  • The "25% Buffer": This 25% is intended to cover minor illnesses, personal emergencies, and casual absences.

2. Types of Leaves for Students

Leave Type Purpose Documentation Required
Medical Leave Serious illness or injury requiring prolonged rest. Medical certificate from a doctor.
Sports Leave Participating in state/national/college sports. Approval letter from the Sports Teacher/Coordinator/HOD.
Hackathon & Technical Event Leave Participating in Hackathon. Invitation letter from Hackathon Institute and/or approval from the Mentor/Coordinator.
Cultural Event Leave Participating in state/national/college Cultural Event. Written application (often signed by a Cultural Coordinator) and Approved by Counsellor/HOD.
Academic Leave Participating in fests, seminars, or workshops. Invitation letter and/or any proof of Participation like Certificate.
Event Duty Organizing/Volunteering for College. Written application (often signed by a Faculty Coordinator).

3. Application & Approval Process

  • Prior Approval: For planned leaves (events/sports/academic), students must apply at least 24-48 hours in advance.
  • Emergency Reporting: In case of sudden illness, the student or parent should notify the Counsellor/HOD via email/WhatsApp/SMS on the first day of absence.
  • Post-Leave Submission: Medical certificates and proof of absence must usually be submitted within 7 working days of returning to campus.
